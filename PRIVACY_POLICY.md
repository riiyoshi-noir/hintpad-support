# Privacy Policy - HintPad

Last updated: February 8, 2026

## Overview

HintPad is a note-taking application for macOS. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

HintPad does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes and data you create are stored locally on your device only.

## Third-Party Services

HintPad does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Network Access

HintPad does not require or use network access. The app functions entirely offline.

## Data Storage

All data (notes, highlights, hint notes, sub-pages) is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on this repository.
